How to Play

  • Arrow Keys: Drive the truck (Up/Down for acceleration, Left/Right for steering)
  • W/S: Adjust camera height
  • A/D: Adjust camera distance
  • C: Toggle chat window
  • GOAL: Crash into obstacles for points and perform epic jumps!
